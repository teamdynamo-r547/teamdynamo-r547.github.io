Introduction

Welcome to the course!

How are you feeling about working in a group? Did it bring you great joy or did your heart sink? Your previous experiences may or may not have resulted in a good product. Even if they did, you may still have felt the pain of poor behaviors from your group members or from yourself.​

Many times, people may not have had the needed training or resources to plan for a successful group experience. What do successful groups do? They define a set of expectations for how the members will:​

  • create an effective culture to handle team dynamics​
  • be accountable for project tasks​
  • communicate with other team members​

To define these expectation, your group will develop a Contract of Collaboration and a Communication Plan through two phases: ​

Phase 1: Each of you will individually complete this training which provides the preparation for that meeting to help you identify your expectations and how you like to work.​ Phase 2: In that first meeting after this training, your group will use the worksheet you will complete in this training to develop a Contract of Collaboration and a Communication Plan to guide the group process throughout the semester. ​ Click on "Next" to learn more.​
