Contract of Collaboration

My Suggestions for Group Rules

Your group has been tasked with creating a Contract of Collaboration that includes your group consensus on how your group will function. This Contract of Collaboration will come out of your first group meeting. As a final preparation to bring to that meeting, each group member will create a list of suggested "rules."

To create this list, reflect on your own previous experiences in group work, your hopes and dreams for this group, your own working style and those styles that others may bring to the group, as well as successful practices you encountered in your background readings. Think about different categories/skills of group work that are important to you (such as participation, listening, etc) and what rules you might put under those categories.
