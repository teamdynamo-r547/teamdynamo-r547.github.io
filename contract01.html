Contract of Collaboration

Getting started

Scenario: To successfully navigate this project, your group needs to develop both a Contract of Collaboration and a Communication Plan. You know that well-formed collaboration and communication plans will help the group successfully complete this literature review project. Everyone on this team has agreed to actively participate in developing these plans.

Let's begin with preparing your portion for that first group meeting where you will work together to develop your group's Contract of Collaboration. This will entail answering questions around several aspects of working in a group such as: Group purpose, task delegation, climate, participation, listening, disagreement, and how the meetings will be run (Parker, 2006).

Building Your Worksheet

As you progress through this training, you will use the activities to complete the Contract of Collaboration Worksheet that you will bring to your first group meeting.

Open the Worksheet in a new window on your computer or print it out so that you can add to it as you progress through the exercises.
