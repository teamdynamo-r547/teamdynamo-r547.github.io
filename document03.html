Document Collaboration

Option 1: Google Drive

Google Drive is a fast and easy way to immediately create documents and spreadsheets – as well as store PDFs and images. It works completely in your web browser – there’s nothing you need to install.

Watch this introduction about Google Drive to get started:

Here are some pros and cons about choosing to use Google Drive:

Pros

  1. Everyone at Indiana University already has a free account
  2. You have virtually unlimited space
  3. Works in the browser with nothing to install

Cons

  1. Possible conversion issues if your documents must be Word/PowerPoint
