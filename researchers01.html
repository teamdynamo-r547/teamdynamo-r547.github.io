Contract of Collaboration

What the Researchers Say

Understanding components of successful groups is key to helping your group be as successful as possible. Each member in your group has agreed to do some common background reading to be able to identify from the research specific practices of successful teams and then evaluate your group priorities.

Complete these readings and take notes on the specific practices you think are most important for your group project work on the "What the Researchers Say" Worksheet.
