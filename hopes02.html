My hopes and fears: Brainstorming & Synthesizing

Here are some suggestions for identifying your hopes and dreams.

  • Review those previous group experiences from the previous activities
  • Brainstorm
    • For the positive ones, think about what you hope to see happen in your new group as well
    • For the negative ones, think about what you fear will happen in your new group
  • Synthesize
    • Determine to greatest hope and your worst fear from you brainstorming step
  • Write your top hope and top fear in the “My hopes and fears” section of the Contract of Collaboration Worksheet.
