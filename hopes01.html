Contract of Collaboration

My hopes and fears

Scenario: Next, we move on to looking at the full spectrum of how a group experience can turn out. On the one end, there is your greatest hope and on the other, there is your greatest fear. Thus, in preparation for your first group meeting, you each have also agreed to bring your greatest hope for the group and your deepest fear.

You have already thought about your previous group work in terms of positive and negative experiences. Here are examples of what might go into this section of the Contract of Collaboration Worksheet. Once you review these, complete the activities that follow.

Examples

Here is an example of what might be entered in the Contract of Collaboration Worksheet in the "My Hopes and Fears" section.

Hope

  • My greatest hope is that everyone does their share of the work on time

Fear

  • My greatest fear is that I will do all the work
