Communication Plan

Real-Time Communication Option 3: Google Hangouts

Hangouts allows your team to meet simultaneously and use your computer's camera and microphone to conduct your team meeting.

Watch this introduction about Google Hangouts to get started:

Here are some pros and cons about choosing to use Google Hangouts:

Pros

  1. Everyone at Indiana University already has a free account
  2. Very easy to install and fast to start a call

Cons

  1. Google has a lot of communication products (Meet, Hangouts, Duo) and it's easy to get confused
  2. Meetings cannot be recorded
  3. No annotation tools
  4. No screen-sharing capabilities
