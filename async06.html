Asynchronous Communication

Option 4: Group SMS

Sending a group text to your team might seem like the best way to get started, but it can often become confusing. Students who use iOS have a much different SMS experience than those who use Android â€“ and often, messages can become delayed or lost.

Images and links are treated differently in the two main mobile platforms, so messages will be treated inconsistently. Also, SMS threads are difficult to search so important communications can be lost in the jumble very quickly.

Here are some pros and cons about choosing to use Group SMS:

Pros

  1. Free and available to everyone
  2. Familiar to all

Cons

  1. Difficult to search
  2. iOS vs Android means some messages can be delayed or lost
  3. Hard to provide a transcript or "pin" specific messages as important
