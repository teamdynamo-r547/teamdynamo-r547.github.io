Here are some pros and cons about choosing to use Zoom:

Pros

  1. Everyone at Indiana University already has a free account
  2. You can record meetings to share or review later
  3. Screen sharing is relatively easy

Cons

  1. It requires a lot of bandwidth
  2. There are annotation tools and a whiteboard, but they are a little clunky
