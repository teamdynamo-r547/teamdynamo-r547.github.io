Contract of Collaboration

My experiences with group work

Begin this process by reflecting upon your previous experiences with group work. To facilitate collaborative work, it helps to talk about group dynamics with the group members. Each person in your group has agreed to meet together and discuss their opinions of collaborative work to understand where you each are coming from as you team up. To help prepare for this, identify some good and bad group practices you have experienced. To do this, first look at these examples, and then complete the brainstorming and synthesizing activities that follow.

Examples

Here is an example of what might be entered in the Contract of Collaboration Worksheet (link worksheet) for this “Group Experiences” section.

Worked Well

  • In a bio class, my lab partner and I each did our share of the work and completed the assignment
  • In a history class, our group met once a week and kept each other motivated

Did Not Work Well

  • We could never find a time to meet!
  • Two people disagreed about everything
