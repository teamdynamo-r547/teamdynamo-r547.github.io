Contract of Collaboration

My Working Style

Group members all have different styles of working and interacting. Are you aware of yours? Take a look at this video and see if you can recognize yourself and others you have worked with before.

By knowing your own working style, you might also realize how others may be different. These different styles can actually make a group stronger on the whole, even if not everyone seems “compatible” at first. Take stock of your own working style to help you realize how you best can contribute for when you start talking with your group by completing the questionnaire that follows.

Study Habit Questionnaire

The Study Habit Questionnaire was created by Janet Mannheimer Zydney of the University of Cinncinnati and is used to help form effective groups.

  • Ergulec, F., & Zydney, J. M. (2019). A design case for implementing a collaborative strategy for online teams. International Journal of Designs for Learning, 10(1), 25 - 34.
