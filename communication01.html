Commmunication Plan

Introduction

A communication plan is an important factor in your team's success. It will set clear guidelines on how to share information, keep track of team responsibilities, and ensure that all team members are aware of the status of your project's components.

You'll communicate with your group in different ways. Sometimes the group needs to come together at the same time to discuss major issues or map out ideas. Other times you might just want to leave feedback for each other or ask questions that aren't urgent. Both types of communication are important, so you'll decide on the preferred way to communicate both in real-time and asynchronously.

Finally, you'll work together to establish a routine for collaborating on the documents your team will produce.
